About J. Steven Southwell, P.A.

Southwell Law provides strategic counsel and hands-on representation in real estate matters — from residential closings and title review to commercial transactions, leases, and dispute resolution. Our aim is clear: protect your property interests and close deals efficiently.

The Firm

A boutique practice focused exclusively on real estate law. We take pride in tailored, practical solutions for buyers, sellers, landlords, tenants, lenders, and developers.

Approach

We emphasize clarity, transparent fees, and proactive communication. Whether drafting contracts, curing title issues, or negotiating leases — we aim to reduce risk and get transactions closed.

Meet the Attorney

J. Steven Southwell has decades of experience in Florida real estate law and a practical approach to client service.

Practice Areas
  • Residential & Commercial Transactions
  • Title & Escrow Counsel
  • Lease Drafting & Review
  • Dispute Resolution & Litigation

Credentials

Bar admission: Florida. Member of local and state bar associations; continuing legal education in real estate and title law.

Client Focus

We work with individual buyers and sellers, brokers, landlords, and small-to-medium developers.
